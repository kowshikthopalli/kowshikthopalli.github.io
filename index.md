
## Hi, my name is Kowshik Thopalli and welcome to my page. 

I am a PhD student in the [Geometric Media lab](https://pavanturaga.com/geometric-media-lab/) in Arizona State University. My advisor is [Dr. Pavan Turaga](https://pavanturaga.com/). 
I did my Master's thesis in the same lab and my thesis can be found [here](https://repository.asu.edu/attachments/194121/content/Thopalli_asu_0010N_17544.pdf). 
My broad research area is in Computer Vision. I am interested in Computer Vision applications using deep learning and also leveraging from the vast theory of Topological Data Analysis, Differential Geometry and Optimal Transport.
